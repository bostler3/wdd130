The United States of America (USA) is a vast country located in North America, bordered by Canada to the north, Mexico to the south, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. It consists of 50 states, with Washington, D.C. as its capital. Known for its diverse landscapes, the USA boasts stunning natural wonders like the Grand Canyon, Yellowstone National Park, and the Great Lakes. The country is a melting pot of cultures, with influences from around the world shaping its unique identity. It has a rich history marked by significant events such as the Declaration of Independence in 1776, the Civil War, and the Civil Rights Movement. The USA is also renowned for its contributions to science, technology, and the arts, as well as being a global leader in economics and politics. (Microsoft Copilot)
